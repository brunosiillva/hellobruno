E-commerce Platform

Full-Stack Development

Project Overview

A comprehensive e-commerce solution built for a growing retail business. The platform handles inventory management, order processing, payment integration, and customer analytics with real-time updates and secure transactions.

The Challenge

The client needed a scalable platform that could handle high traffic during peak seasons while maintaining fast load times and secure payment processing. The existing system was outdated and couldn't support their growth.
