About

Passionate about creating digital experiences that delight and truly work. With a collaborative, organized, and agile approach, I turn products into intuitive, accessible, people-centered solutions. To me, design goes beyond aesthetics, it's about solving problems and making technology more human.

At Nio, I was responsible for NioDS, a multiplatform, accessible design system for web and app. I built an AI agent pipeline that lets me sustain the consistency and the volume of documentation, code, and specification that would normally require an entire team.

At RD Station, I led the evolution and scalability of Tangram, the design system behind the company's products. The work raised the consistency and usability of the interfaces and delivered productivity gains for the teams, reducing rework and speeding up delivery, which made it possible to ship solutions faster and with higher quality.

As a Product Designer with a hybrid profile, I move fluidly between the technical and the strategic, bridging communication between design and engineering and making sure the solutions we design are not only desirable and viable, but also executable and sustainable.

I believe consistent products are born from collaboration between design, engineering, and business, and it's at that intersection, between the technical and the strategic, that I like to work.
